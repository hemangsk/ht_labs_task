India and Alcohol Use Disorders

How far we are from mastering the art of moderate-controlled drinking plan.

A ccording to the report of World Health Organisation, one in twenty Indian males have an addiction or alcohol related disorder.

The harmful psychological effects of uncontrolled alcohol consumption leads to serious threats to the society. Alcoholism, also known as Alcohol Use Disorders is quite evidently the 'most economical mechanism' to cause chaos in the Indian society.
